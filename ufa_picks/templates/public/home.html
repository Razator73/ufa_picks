Welcome to UFA Picks

This is a site to help manage our picks for the UFA. Just as a fun little game.

In the 2026 season, games are scored as follows:

  • 3 points for correctly guessing the winning team
  • Up to 2 bonus points (1 point per team) for guessing their exact final score
  • 1 bonus point for guessing the exact margin of victory

At the end of the regular season, your lowest scoring week is automatically dropped! The playoffs will follow through to crowning a champion.
