Welcome to UFA Picks

This is a site to help manage our picks for the UFA. Just as a fun little game.

You can score when you guess the correct winner with up to 3 bonus points for:

  • Getting a team's final score right (up to two points)
  • Getting the correct margin of victory (or closest to it)

Making each game worth a total of 4 points.
