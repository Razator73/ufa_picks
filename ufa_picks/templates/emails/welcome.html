Thanks for joining our UFA Picks game. Each week you'll pick the winners and scores of UFA games — the more you get right, the more points you earn. The picks each week will lock at the start of the first game of the week. So you can't edit other games in the week once that first game starts. Once picks are locked you'll be able to click on the game and see what the other players picked.

You can also follow other users to create a friends ranking. You will be able to see how you stack up against players you follow. Feel free to invite your friends to join. This is just for fun and the prizes will be for overall placements.

How Scoring Works

  • 3 points for correctly picking the winning team
  • Up to 2 bonus points (1 per team) for guessing the exact final score
  • 1 bonus point for guessing the exact margin of victory

At the end of the regular season your lowest-scoring week is automatically dropped, so one rough week won't sink you.

Prizes

  • 1st place — Jersey of your choice
  • 2nd & 3rd place — Utah Jazz or Utah Mammoth hat
